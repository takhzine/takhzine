Accumulating
Twenty million official archival documents tracing the history of the French and Spanish colonial rules in Morocco are hoarded away from home. Sixty eight years after independence, the country is not in possession of almost four million of these documents, making it difficult to better understand and fill in the gaps of key episodes in its history.
